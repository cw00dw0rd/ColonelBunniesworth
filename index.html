Colonel Bunniesworth: A Journey Through Space & Time

In this adventure you are playing as Colonel Bunniesworth on a quest to bilk the bilker. The carrots of your homeworld Lagos were stolen for their magical properties and you are here to reclaim them. Beware, for the lair of this curly wolf is laden with mines set to blow you to bits at the slightest touch.

To Walk or Run: use   W     A     S     D   or the arrow keys
To Jump: use the up arrow key or the space bar
To Descend: use   S   or the down arrow key
Music:   O   to pause   P   to play


Collect all carrots to move to the next level, don't trust the ground beneath your feet, and avoid the mines at all costs!
